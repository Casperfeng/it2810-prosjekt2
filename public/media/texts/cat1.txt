Cat
By Marilyn Singer
https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poems/147301/cat-5b3ba51bf2687

I prefer
warm fur,
a perfect fire
to lie beside,
a cozy lap
where I can nap,
an empty chair
when she's not there.
I want heat
     on my feet
     on my nose
     on my hide.
No cat I remember
dislikes December
     inside.
